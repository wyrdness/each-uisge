## Comparative Mythology

### Celtic Water Horse Variants
- **Kelpie** — Freshwater river/loch variant (Scotland)
- **Aughisky** — Irish variant of each-uisge
- **Ceffyl Dŵr** — Welsh water horse
- **Cabyll-Ushtey** — Manx (Isle of Man) water horse
- **Nuckelavee** — Orcadian sea demon (even more malevolent)

### European Water Spirits
- Germanic *Nix/Nixie*
- Scandinavian *Nøkk*
- Slavic water spirits
- Comparative analysis in:
  - Ashliman, D.L. (2004). *Folk and Fairy Tales: A Handbook*. Greenwood Press.
  - Leeming, D. (2005). *The Oxford Companion to World Mythology*. Oxford University Press.
